Discovery before roadmap

I don't start with features. Jobs-to-be-Done research, user interviews, and behavioral data validate the right problem before a single ticket is written. Wasted sprints are a strategy failure, not an engineering one.

Marquee case study

★ Series C · $100M Raise · $500M Valuation
Kitchen United — Scaling a Ghost Kitchen Marketplace to Series C
Senior Product Manager · Sept 2019 – May 2023 · Pasadena, CA · Google Ventures Portfolio
Multi-sided MarketplaceB2B/B2C SaaSDelivery APIsiOS · Android · KioskHeap · Segment
The Problem

A three-sided marketplace — ghost kitchen operators, restaurant brands, consumers — fragmented across iOS, Android, kiosks, and delivery platforms. Cart abandonment high, ordering workflows broken, platform lacked scalability needed to attract Series C investors.

My Approach

Built a data-driven experimentation culture using Heap, Segment, and GA. Deep discovery with operators, consumers, and delivery partners surfaced friction across ordering, inventory, and fulfillment. Directed 12+ cross-functional team members across Engineering, Design, and QA.

What We Shipped

Multi-restaurant ordering flows, real-time inventory sync, kitchen capacity management, and API integrations with Uber Eats, DoorDash, and Grubhub — across 30+ physical locations. Checkout optimized via session analysis and A/B testing.
